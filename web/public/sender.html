Sendo — Sender

Enter the 4-character key from your Kobo/Kindle, then upload an e-book.

Open this page on your Kobo or Kindle e-reader. A unique 4-character key will appear above, along with a QR code for quick access.

On another device, enter the key and upload an e-book. Once uploaded, the e-book will appear here as a download link on your e-reader.

Uploaded e-books are stored temporarily while your e-reader is connected. They are deleted shortly after you disconnect, refresh the page, or close Wi-Fi.

Supported formats: EPUB, MOBI, PDF, TXT. Kobo users may use Kepubify conversion, Kindle users may use KindleGen for MOBI compatibility.
